# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

AI Replacing Jobs provides an informational AI job exposure checker. The v1 checker runs in your browser and does not require an account.

## Information We Process

The checker asks for job title, industry, experience level, work type, daily tasks, optional custom task notes, AI usage level, and optional region. In v1, this input is used in the browser to calculate an estimate and is not saved by the site.

## Analytics

The site may use privacy-conscious analytics in the future to understand page views and tool actions such as completed checks or copied results. We do not need raw job titles or custom task text for analytics.

## Cookies

The public v1 site does not require login cookies. Some template-level or hosting functionality may use technical cookies if optional features are enabled later.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@AIreplacingjobs.org.

## Disclaimer

The checker is informational. It estimates AI exposure and is not a guaranteed job loss prediction, legal advice, financial advice, or career counseling.
